Daniel's Code Blog Entry #3

What was so hard about this project?

Where do I begin? Prior to this assignment, I had zero experience with JavaScript. I do remember having tried to teach myself JavaScript way back when my senior year of high school, but I also had no experience with coding--so the experience in a loosely typed langauge did not go over so well. This time, I had extensive experience with object oriented programming comming into this class, which came in handy when working with the DOM. But having the confidence to get started because I had those terrible flashbacks to trying to teach myself is what limited me on this assingment.

What was helpful in completing this assignment?

My background in object oriented programming was helpful in this assignment. Also, Stack Overflow was a great resource because there were a few functions I didn't know existed in JavaScript that helped me complete the assignment.

What are two things you had to Google?

One thing I had to google was how I could unhide elements. It was so trivial, but I didn't know how to necessarily utilize JavaScript--so I had no idea that I could change CSS properties by way of event listeners. Overall, web development is really growing on me.

What are your overall thoughts on the assignment?

My overall thoughts on this assigmnet is that I had a lot of fun figuring out these issues I ran into due to my lack of experience working with JavaScript.
